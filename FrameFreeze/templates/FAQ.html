Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using AirSwipe for gesture-based control of games, tools, and productivity features.

AirSwipe • Gesture Control • Help

General FAQs

1. What is AirSwipe?
+
AirSwipe is a gesture-based control platform that lets you interact with apps using hand movements instead of clicks.
2. How does AirSwipe work?
+
It uses your device’s camera to detect hand gestures and convert them into actions.
3. Do I need any special device?
+
No, any device with a working camera is enough.
4. Does AirSwipe collect personal data?
+
No, all gesture detection is processed locally and we don’t store any personal data.
5. Is AirSwipe free to use?
+
Yes, our basic features are free.
6. Where can I use AirSwipe?
+
You can use AirSwipe on our website for games, tools, and productivity features.
7. Is AirSwipe safe?
+
Yes, it does not record or save your video feed.
8. Do I need to create an account?
+
Some features require login, but most gesture tools can be used directly.
9. Which browsers support AirSwipe?
+
AirSwipe works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
10. Does AirSwipe work on mobile?
+
Yes, but performance may vary depending on camera quality and browser support.

Gesture Control FAQs

11. What gestures do you support?
+
We support taps, swipes, pointer movement, drawing motions, and game-specific gestures.
12. How do I activate gesture mode?
+
Click on “Enable Motion Sensor” on any supported feature.
13. Does AirSwipe track my whole body?
+
No, AirSwipe only tracks your hand and finger movements.
14. Why is my gesture not detected?
+
Low lighting, poor camera quality, or an awkward camera angle may affect detection. Make sure your hand is clearly visible.
15. Do I need to keep my hand in the frame?
+
Yes, your hand should always stay inside the camera view for consistent tracking.
16. Which hand can I use?
+
You can use either hand for most features unless mentioned otherwise.
17. Can I customize gestures?
+
Gesture customization will be added soon in upcoming updates.
18. How far should I sit from the camera?
+
About 1–2 feet away from the camera usually gives the best accuracy.
19. Can AirSwipe detect both hands?
+
Yes, it can detect both hands, but most games currently use single-hand mode.
20. Can I disable gesture control anytime?
+
Yes, click “Disable Motion Sensor” to turn off gesture control.

Games FAQs

21. Which games are available on AirSwipe?
+
Car Race, Table Tennis, Rock-Paper-Scissors, Sudoku, One-Line Draw, and Color Switch.
22. Do all games support gesture control?
+
Yes, every game on AirSwipe is motion-sensor enabled.
23. Why is my game lagging?
+
Low lighting, slow internet, or a low-performance device can cause delays in gesture response or gameplay.
24. Can I play using keyboard instead?
+
Yes, most games support both keyboard and gesture options.
25. Is multiplayer mode available?
+
Currently no, but we are working on adding multiplayer features in future versions.
26. Can I customize the game themes?
+
Game themes follow our AirSwipe neon style and may be updated later with more customization options.
27. Why is the ball/car/racket not moving?
+
Make sure “Enable Motion Sensor” is turned on and your hand is clearly visible in the camera frame.
28. Does my background affect gesture recognition?
+
Very bright, cluttered, or patterned backgrounds may reduce accuracy. A plain background works best.
29. Do I need a high-end camera for gaming?
+
No, a normal laptop or phone camera is usually enough, though higher resolution can improve tracking quality.
30. Can I play the games full-screen?
+
Yes, most games support a full-screen mode so you can enjoy an immersive experience.

Advanced & Settings FAQs

31. Do I need to calibrate my hand before starting?
+
Yes, AirSwipe may ask you to show your hand once to set the gesture boundary before starting.
32. Can I adjust the sensitivity of gesture recognition?
+
Yes, sensitivity settings are available in the Motion Control Settings panel.
33. Does AirSwipe work in low light?
+
Gesture accuracy decreases in low light; better lighting significantly improves performance.
34. Why does the screen say “Hand Not Detected”?
+
Your hand may be outside the camera frame, too far away, or the lighting might be poor. Adjust your position and lighting.
35. Can I use AirSwipe while wearing gloves?
+
Thin gloves may work, but thick or dark gloves can reduce detection accuracy.
36. Does AirSwipe store my gesture videos?
+
No. Gestures are processed live only; we do not record or save video.
37. Can multiple people use gesture controls at the same time?
+
No, AirSwipe is designed to detect only one user’s hand at a time for accurate control.
38. Can I use AirSwipe without enabling the camera?
+
No, camera access is required for gesture detection to work.
39. Why is the motion sensor showing delayed response?
+
This can happen due to low lighting, slow device processing, or camera lag. Try improving lighting or closing heavy apps.
40. Which hand should I use for gesture games?
+
You can use either hand; AirSwipe will detect whichever is visible in the frame.
41. Does AirSwipe support both horizontal and vertical gestures?
+
Yes, it detects swipes, pinches, taps, circles, and other directional gestures in both directions.
42. Why does my gesture sometimes trigger the wrong action?
+
Background objects, very fast movements, or partial hand visibility may confuse the sensor. Try slower and clearer gestures.
43. Can I resize the gesture control window?
+
Yes, you can drag and resize the camera preview panel where supported.
44. Does background colour affect detection?
+
Yes, very bright or patterned backgrounds may reduce accuracy. A neutral background is recommended.
45. Can AirSwipe recognise finger count (1 finger, 2 finger, 3 finger actions)?
+
Yes, finger-count detection is supported for specific features like One-Line Draw and other gesture-based tools.
46. Does AirSwipe work on college WiFi?
+
Yes, it works on any internet connection. Gestures run locally on your device, not on a remote server.
47. Can I customise the theme or background?
+
The neon theme is the default. Custom backgrounds will be added later as part of future updates.
48. Why does my camera turn off when switching pages?
+
Some browsers auto-reset camera permissions when you navigate. You may need to re-enable camera access on the new page.
49. Can I use AirSwipe on a tablet?
+
Yes, tablets with good-quality cameras work well with AirSwipe.
50. What happens if my camera has low resolution?
+
Gesture detection will still work, but accuracy may decrease slightly with very low-resolution cameras.
51. How do I report issues or suggest new gesture features?
+
Use the “Feedback / Report Issue” button available in the footer to share bugs, ideas, or feature requests.

Still stuck? Reach out via the Feedback button on the main page.
